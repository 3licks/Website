What?

We're a non-profit group bringing a hacker camp (like CCC, HAR, and toorcamp) to the UK in 2012. It'll be a bit like a cross between a music festival and a conference with a wide range of talks on geeky subjects. For updates, follow us on Twitter.

For your ticket price you'll get a place to camp, power, internet, talks, workshops, and much more geekery.

When?

Friday 31st August - Sunday 2nd September 2012

Where?

Pineham Park, Milton Keynes, UK

Count Me In!

To help us get an idea of numbers, we'd appreciate it if you filled in this quick form to give us an idea of interest. There's no obligation to come and we'll only use your email address to contact you about the camp.

Get Involved

EMF is a volunteer-run effort. If you want to help out:

  • Follow us on Twitter
  • Sign up to the mailing list
  • Join us on irc.freenode.net #ukhackcamp
  • Read our wiki
