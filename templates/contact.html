Contact us

Want to get in contact? Here's how:

  • Email us. This is the best way to get hold of us with specific requests, questions and any offers of help (although please check the FAQ and Get Involved pages first).
  • @emfcamp is our twitter id and is a good source of news as to what's going on.
  • The Facebook page has a good list of who's planning on coming (in addition to 'Who's Coming').
  • Our mailing list contains a lot of discussion as to how the camp will be run as well as being the first place for news. It also acts as a staging post for people to organise their own things within the camp.
  • Our IRC channel (irc.freenode.net #ukhackcamp) is where we chat and run things on a day-to-day basis. Feel free to drop in and get to know us.
  • The wiki contains all our organisational information, minutes, team notes as well as projects pages. If you want to get a feel of what's going on this is an excellent first stop.
  • Our blog features news on the status of the camp as well as reports on what we're up to.
  • Answer our Call for Participation. If you want to give a talk, run a workshop or host an event this is where you need to go.
