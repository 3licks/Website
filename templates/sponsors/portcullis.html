Portcullis

Portcullis is delighted to be sponsoring EMF 2014 at Bletchley for what promises to be their biggest and best event ever.

Since our formation in 1986, Portcullis has developed into a widely recognized and respected provider of Information Security services with the strong foundation that comes from being an independent, mature and financially stable Company.

Portcullis’ excellent reputation stems from our Security Testing Services, launched back in 1996, which flourished into the professional and high quality services that our clients benefit from today. This is further endorsed by Portcullis’ array of industry accreditations and the numerous CHECK Team Leaders / Members and CREST Application / Infrastructure Consultants we have. This is testament to the investment Portcullis makes in its highly skilled workforce, their training and our research and development.

As a well established provider of Information security we understand the importance of networking and sharing ideas and information within our industry. This not only helps us fight cyber crime but also allows our industry and those related to grow and reach new areas. That’s why Portcullis is excited to sponsor Electromagnetic Field this year having attended in previous years and found it such a fantastic place to share ideas and learn.

We welcome people to come visit us at our village this year and within the, you’ll find our location on the EMF wiki and you’re sure to see our Portcullis flags flying high!
