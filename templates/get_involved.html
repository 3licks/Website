Help us out

EMF is a volunteer-run effort. If you want to help we need people both now and during the camp to help organise and run things.

We have several open positions in our organising teams. If you're interested in any of them please email us or get in contact elsewhere.

If organising isn't your thing we'll need volunteers to help run the camp itself. We're looking to fill positions such as:

  • Bar staff
  • Gate duty
  • Car parking
  • Set-up/tear-down
  • Running the info desk
  • A/V stage hand work
  • Stewarding

Please note that all our volunteers (including the organising team, but with the exception of first-aiders who will always be on call) are required to buy a ticket.
