Electromagnetic Pulse (EMP)

EMF is founding a new series of small events called EMP, or Electromagnetic Pulse.

These are bi-monthly small meetups that give the wider community a regular place to meet up between EMFs, to talk about and show projects, hang out, and plan for the future. They'll also be a great place to get involved with EMF organisation if you're interested.

While the first events are likely to be in cities where some of the current EMF team live, we would like to see them spread quickly - and anyone can start one! If you're interested in founding an EMP meeting in your city, please get in touch.

The first official event will be early this year in London, however we expect to have other events starting around the country very shortly afterwards - and hopefully all over the world!

This page will be updated with a list of EMP meetings and further details about the events in the near future.

If you'd like to be notified when the EMP events start, please sign up to our notification mailing list.
