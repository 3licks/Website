Straw bale hire

If you're planning to form a village, we can help fit out your communal area by hiring straw bales for the duration of the event.

Bales will be delivered from our local supplier, and will be available for you to use by Thursday evening. Any intact bales will be collected at the end of the event for agricultural use.
