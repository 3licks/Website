Electromagnetic Field

(a planned 3-day UK hacker camp in summer 2012)

What?

We're a non-profit group planning on bringing a hacker camp (like CCC, HAR, and toorcamp) to the UK in 2012. For updates, follow us on Twitter.

Count Me In!

To help us get an idea of numbers, we'd appreciate it if you filled in this quick form to give us an idea of interest. There's no obligation to come and we'll only use your email address to contact you about the camp.

Get Involved

EMF is a volunteer-run non-profit effort. If you want to help out:

  • Follow us on Twitter
  • Sign up to the mailing list
  • Join us on irc.freenode.net #uk-hack-camp
